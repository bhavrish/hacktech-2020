Tips For Avoiding The Coronavirus

  • Avoid events with many attendees (>50).
  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Try to stay home if you're sick.
  • Carry some hand sanitizer or wet wipes and clean surfaces you might touch as well as your hands.
  • Wash your hands when possible, with soap and water, for at least 20 seconds.

Tips For Avoiding Confrontations

  • Travel in areas where people can see you.
  • Be cautious- notice your surroundings and don't use your phone in public.
  • Have a friend travel with you since the probability of getting approached lowers substantially.
  • Cover your face with sunglasses, a mask, and maybe even a hood.
  • Try to avoid people who appear agitated.
